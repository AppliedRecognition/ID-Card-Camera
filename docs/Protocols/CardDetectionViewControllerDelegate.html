  • qualityOfImage(_:)

    Called when an image is straightened

    Implement in your delegate to determine the image quality. By default the quality is determined by the sharpness of the image. However, sharpness detection is only available on iOS 13 and newer. If you wish to keep the sharpness detection and implement your own image quality scoring for iOS 12 and older return nil on iOS 13 and your score on iOS 12 and older.
